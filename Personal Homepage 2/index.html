About Me

My name is Antonius Torode. I have always tried to be the jack of all traits and if something needs done I will learn to do it myself. I very much enjoy trying to look at things with a perspective that no others have before. Most of my pre-university knowledge was self taught (Mathematics, programming, video editing, graphic design, etc). While growing up, I always enjoyed chess and anything to do with technology which has given me a good foundation for where I am at now. Eventually I hope to have the opportunity to research at some of the larger universities, or perhaps even in Antarctica (The south pole station has always fascinated me), but I will most likely plan on teaching at some point in my future, as I have been told I am good at tutoring and helping others.

My hobbies have always spread over a wide spectrum. In high school I spent the majority of my time playing World of Warcraft. After high school I switched to learning new things like graphic design and programming. As of recently, I have started to develop a healthier lifestyle through diet and exercise. A lot of my time is spent cooking my own meals which I try to do for myself and others every day. I have tried to develop a diet that consists of a large variety of whole foods that both tastes good and provides balanced nutrition. I am a large believer in using calesthenics (body weight exercises) to get in shape and so recently I have been training to grow in my capabilities with that.
