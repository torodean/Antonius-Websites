About Antonius Torode

My name is Antonius Torode. I just finished both physics and mathematics degrees at Michigan State University. I am remaining a student at MSU during the summer of 2018 to continue my research in nuclear astrophysics. Alongside my research, I work at the Helpdesk as a student computing assistant for the physics and astronomy departments here at MSU.

My current plans include to taking a year off of college to study other areas that fascinate me. At the moment I am planning to attend Ambassador Bible College in Ohio while exploring other self-driven areas of study such as health and nutrition. After this 9 month program I plan to continue my education further and obtain a Ph.D in physics.

I very much enjoy trying to look at things with a perspective that no others have before. Most of my pre-university knowledge was self taught (Mathematics, programming, video editing, graphic design, etc). While growing up, I always enjoyed chess and anything to do with technology which has given me a good foundation for where I am at now. Eventually I hope to have the opportunity to research at some of the larger universities, or perhaps even in Antarctica (The south pole station has always fascinated me), but I will most likely plan on teaching at some point in my future, as I have been told I am good at tutoring and helping others.

My hobbies have always spread over a wide spectrum. In high school I spent the majority of my time playing World of Warcraft. After high school I switched to learning new things like graphic design and programming. As of recently, I have started to develop a healthier lifestyle through diet and exercise. A lot of my time is spent cooking my own meals which I try to do for myself and others every day. I have tried to develop a diet that consists of a large variety of whole foods that both tastes good and provides balanced nutrition. I am a large believer in using calesthenics (body weight exercises) to get in shape and so recently I have been training to grow in my capabilities with that.
