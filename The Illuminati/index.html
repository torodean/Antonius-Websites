About Illuminatti WoW

The Illuminati (alliance) and Little Illuminati (horde) are World of Warcraft guilds stationed on the US-Dalaran server. We have implemented a unique zero chat spam recruiting system that has attracted mature and fun individuals. We have a casual and fair hierarchy system, guild repairs for all members and over 750 guild achivement points. The guild master has over 10 years of WoW experience. Our main form of communication is through in game guild chat or our personal discord server where both alliance and horde guilds can communicate! Our discord includes a fully functional music bot (Illumiboti) where players can relax and listen to music while questing, leveling or raiding.
