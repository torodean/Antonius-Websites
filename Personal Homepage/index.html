ABOUT ME

My name is Antonius Torode. I just finished both physics and mathematics degrees at Michigan State University. I am remaining a student at MSU during the summer of 2018 to continue my research in nuclear astrophysics. Alongside my research, I work at the Helpdesk as a student computing assistant for the physics and astronomy departments here at MSU.

My current plans include to taking a year off of college to study other areas that fascinate me. At the moment I am planning to attend Ambassador Bible College in Ohio while exploring other self-driven areas of study such as health and nutrition. After this 9 month program I plan to continue my education further and obtain a Ph.D in physics.

I very much enjoy trying to look at things with a perspective that no others have before. Most of my pre-university knowledge was self taught (Mathematics, programming, video editing, graphic design). I have always enjoyed chess and anything to do with technology in my free time. Eventually I hope to have the opportunity to research at some of the larger universities, or perhaps even in Antarctica (The south pole station has always fascinated me), but I will most likely plan on teaching at some point in my future, as I have always been good at tutoring.
